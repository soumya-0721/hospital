FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online through our website by filling out the appointment form. You can also call our helpline or visit the hospital directly.

What documents do I need to bring?

Please bring a valid photo ID, previous medical records, and any relevant prescriptions or lab reports.

Do you accept health insurance?

Yes, we accept all major health insurance plans. Please bring your insurance card and we'll assist you with the claim process.

Can I get my medicines delivered?

Yes! Our online pharmacy offers home delivery for all prescription and over-the-counter medicines within the city.

What are your visiting hours?

Outpatient hours are 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. Emergency services are available 24/7, including Sundays and holidays.

How do I access my lab reports online?

Once your tests are completed, you can access your reports through the patient portal. You'll receive a link via SMS and email when your reports are ready.
